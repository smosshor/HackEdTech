EdTech 101

Problem Space

Online learning and educational tools are increasing part of how students of all ages learn new skills. Yet learning through technology offers unique challenges in design, implementation and evaluation.

Opportunities

Opportunity 1: Design tools to help students learn skills

Opportunity 2: Motivate student learning

Opportunity 3: Designing effective assessment

Opportunity 4: Implementing educator professional development

Opportunity 5: Improving educator productivity

Opportunity 6: Making learning accessible

Opportunity 7: Closing opportunity gaps

Opportunity 8: Closing achievement gaps

EdTech Examples

Duolingo

Designing Learning Experiences

If you're interested in creating learning experiences through technology, it's important to know a little about how people learn.

By the end of this section, you will be able to:

    1.) Plan out a learning experience by understanding your students and articulating goals
    2.) Understand research-based instructional strategies and how to use them in any edtech product.
    3.) Be able to evaluate engagement, learning outsomes and broader impact through data.
